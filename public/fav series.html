My Fav Series

Breaking Bad

A high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with cancer partners with a former student to cook meth, plunging into a dangerous underworld. Breaking Bad is a masterfully crafted story of morality, power, and transformation, where every choice has consequences.

Dark

Dark is a mind-bending German sci-fi thriller on Netflix that begins with a missing child and spirals into a complex web of time travel, family secrets, and a mysterious town where past, present, and future collide. With its haunting atmosphere and intricate plot, Dark explores fate, free will, and the ripple effects of human actions across generations.
